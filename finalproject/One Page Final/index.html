About

Legs by Liz was founded in January 2016 by Liz Mulshine, a Jersey born and bred Upstate New Yorker, yoga-enthusiast, nurse and mother of four.
She was introduced to the world of spandex 11 years ago when her daughter asked Liz if she could make her a ski suit, which also led to making one for her daughter's friend, and another friend, and then the whole team.
The yoga pants started in a similar way. She made a pair for herself and then her friends. Advertising was simple as more people started wearing her pants and telling people about them.
As a result, orders are coming in fast. Place your order today!
